When creating Assistance Listings for each of their programs, agencies are offered the opportunity to select one or many "functions" and "sub-functions" from a pre-defined list that best align with the program. The categories and sub-categories shown on this website are "functions" and "sub-functions" self-selected by agencies.

Programs may be assigned to one or more "functions" and / or "sub-functions". The numbers in this visualization and data set may not sum to the cumulative figure displayed on this page, due to the existence of programs in multiple categories and sub-categories. Additionally, the numbers displayed on this page may not represent funding or number of programs associated exclusively with this category and / or sub-category.

In selecting from the list of "functions" and "sub-functions" agencies are not offered guidance on their meaning, nor the number to select. As a result, the number and context of "functions" and "sub-functions" assigned to a program may vary by program. These values are selected by agencies and are published on SAM.gov.
