  \ or a private entity that has as its stated principal organizational purpose or\
  \ goal the conservation, restoration, or preservation of land and natural resources\
  \ in order to address the use or development of real property in the vicinity of,\
  \ or ecologically related to, an Army National Guard installation, or military airspace\

  \ would be incompatible with the mission of the installation;\n(2) preserving habitat\
  \ on the property in a manner that\u2014\n(A) is compatible with environmental requirements;\
  \ and\n(B) may eliminate or relieve current or anticipated environmental restrictions\
  \ that would or might otherwise restrict, impede, or otherwise interfere, whether\
  \ directly or indirectly, with current or anticipated military training, testing,\

  \ is incompatible with the mission of the installation."
