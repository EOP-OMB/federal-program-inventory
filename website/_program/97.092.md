objective: 'To assist States, Indian tribal governments, and communities to reduce
  or eliminate the long-term risk of flood damage to structures insured under the
  National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP) that have had one or more claims for flood
  damages through mitigation activities that are in the best interest of the National
  Flood Insurance Fund (NFIF). This grant program supports the Mitigate component
  of the Presidential Preparedness Directive 8 (PPD-8) in the following ways:  by
  providing funding to states and communities that promotes Long-Term Vulnerability
  Reduction through various mitigation measures including elevating at-risk homes
  or removing structures from flood hazard areas.  Overall, this program also supports
  Community Resilience by reducing the need for emergency services in flood-prone
  areas of the community.'
